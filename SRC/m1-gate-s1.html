Gate 1.TG.1

Module 1 Threshold Check: Structural Approval Gate

Governance Checkpoint -- Not a Scored Assessment

What This Gate Does

Gate 1.TG.1 is the readiness checkpoint at the end of Module 1. It is not a scored test. It is a structured review of your governance record log. All eight records must be present, internally consistent, and coherent with one another. Only then should you advance to Module 2: Engineer the Learning.

Passing the gate means your structural design is ready for the learning engineering work in Module 2. If you find a gap or contradiction, return to the relevant lesson screen and resolve it. Then confirm the checkpoint below.

Four Things This Gate Checks

  1. 1 Record completeness: All eight MRHN governance records (MRHN-D-001 through MRHN-D-008) are present and complete in your record log.
  2. 2 Artifact accuracy: MRHN_DecompositionMap_v2 accurately reflects all optimization and trace audit decisions documented in MRHN-D-007 and MRHN-D-008.
  3. 3 Scope integrity: Your decomposition map contains exactly 48 DC components, all tracing to the 16 scope items established in LC-1. No items were added outside that boundary.
  4. 4 Cross-record coherence: No governance record contradicts a decision made in a later record without documented rationale for the change.
