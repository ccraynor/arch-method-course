Lesson 1.2

Apply Step 2: Bucket the 16 MRHN Scope Items

Step 2
Reference: The expert bucket map is at Screen 2 of this lesson . Do not open it until you have submitted your assignments -- the feedback on this screen will reveal and compare against the expert map.

Two-part task

Part A: Name your four buckets before assigning any scope items. Use language that reflects when in the incident response workflow the items occur -- not what topic they cover.
Part B: Assign all 16 scope items (SI-01 through SI-16) to your four buckets. Every item must be assigned. Items should appear in exactly one bucket. Apply workflow sequence logic, not topic similarity.

A

Name Your Four Buckets

Name each bucket using a phrase that describes its role in the incident response workflow. You will reference these names when assigning scope items below.

B

Assign All 16 Scope Items

Use the dropdown on each row to assign the scope item to one of your four buckets. All 16 items must be assigned before you can submit.
